COMPANY INFORMATION

“Having adventurer spirit in mind, through trading activities with cutting-edged technologies, contribute to new value creation” is the Management Philosophy

As a UK originated technical trading company, AR Brown Co., Ltd. has been involved in cutting-edged technologies in Electronics, Automotive, Chemical, Pharmaceutical, Aviation & Aerospace, Bio and Medical related Industries for more than 70 years.

To materialize the Management Philosophy, trading activities are conducted based on the Management Vision of “by coupling different professionalisms, deliver new values to the market.

As of now, enhancing synergistic operations between the entities in Japan, China, Thailand, India, and South Korea, we continue to take greater challenges pursuing new business environment.
